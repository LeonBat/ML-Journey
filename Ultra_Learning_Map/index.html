Ultra-Learning Map

Machine Learning

Why?

Machine Learning is a field I expect to grow in importance in the age of AI. With this project, I want to understand the fundamentals and the concepts behind it. It also serves as a good foundation for AI in general and allows me to improve my ML engineering skills.

How?

I will follow the principles of ultra-learning as described by Scott Young in his book "Ultralearning: Master Hard Skills, Outsmart the Competition, and Accelerate Your Career". This includes self-directed learning, focusing on high-impact projects, and continuous practice and feedback.

What?

The project will cover a broad range of topics in Machine Learning, including:

  1. Fundamentals of Machine Learning
  2. Supervised and Unsupervised Learning
  3. Neural Networks and Deep Learning
  4. Natural Language Processing
  5. Reinforcement Learning
  6. Model Deployment and Monitoring
